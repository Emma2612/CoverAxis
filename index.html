Our Story

Coveraxis our brand name, where axis means a real or imaginary line on which something rotates. We mean to accompany you throughout your journey.

We are equipped to serve a client base of professionals and accomplished individuals with whom we build solid relationships based on.

Our Services

Employees’ Benefits

As your Insurance Broker, we are committed to assisting your firm in maintaining and preserving its most significant asset, its people. We will assist you by giving them peace of mind.

As a result of their happiness with their coverage, there will be a productivity increase. These plans are beneficial to businesses who provide any of the following services:

  • Group Medical Insurance
  • Group Life Insurance
  • Group Accident Cover
  • Group Travel Insurance
  • Group Pension Plan

Management Liability Insurance

COVERAXIS will develop a complete management liability insurance coverage that will allow your team to function with confidence, based on our experience, knowledge, and broad market access.

  • Professional Indemnity

    Professionals are at risk of being sued for losses or injury to third parties because of their negligence in providing their services. Traditional General Liability Insurance does not cover these claims. We work with companies offering services and products as well as professionals such as doctors, attorneys, architects, engineers, accountants, and other experts to secure their practices.

  • Directors & Officers Liability

    Directors and Officers Liability Insurance is a specific type of management liabilities insurance that covers legal costs and liability to shareholders, bondholders, creditors, and others because of a corporation's or non-profit organization's actions or omissions.

Commercial Insurance

With our comprehensive commercial property and casualty insurance solutions, you can rest assured that your business is protected in the events of physical commercial property loss and liability claims.

  • Business Interruption Insurance

    If your firm is forced to relocate or even close due to an unforeseen catastrophe, such as a fire or natural disaster, Business Interruption insurance, also known as Business Income insurance, will replace lost revenue and protect you from incurring losses.

  • Cyber Insurance

    With work shifting online, new technologies, reliance on IoT services, and stress on a digital-first approach, cyber risk has changed shape and form. Cyber Insurance as a product is now offered by a few insurance companies covering all regular cyber liabilitites.

  • Worker’s Compensation Insurance

    Accidents happen even in the safest workplaces with the most careful employees. Workers' Compensation insurance provides medical benefits, income benefits, death benefits, and rehabilitation benefits if your employees are injured on the job. If an accident occurs on the job, our team will work to protect you and your employees.

  • Commercial Auto Insurance

    Even the most respectable companies are vulnerable to lawsuits. Commercial liability insurance safeguards your assets and serves as a first line of defense against a variety of common claims. It also aids in the continuity of your business in the event of legitimate or fraudulent allegations of negligence or wrongdoing. Our experts go deeper into your company's operations to discover potential risks and exposures.

Personal Insurance

  • Homeowner and Auto

    - Other Structures on Your property
    - Personal Property (i.e., Belongings, furniture etc.)
    - Additional Living Expenses
    - Personal Liability

  • Life and Pension

    “Build your Safety Net”

    COVERAXIS can offer a variety of alternatives to protect your health and help you maintain your earning power so you can provide your family with the financial security they deserve in retirement.
